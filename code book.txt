DATA DICTIONARY - Human Activity Recognition Using Smartphones (Tidy Data)
Activity: 6 types of activity
	LAYING SITTING STANDING WALKING WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS WALKING_UPSTAIRS
Subject: 30 subjects: 1…30
In the following names:
'-XYZ' is used to denote 3-axial signals in the X, Y and Z directions.
‘mean’ denotes this is a mean
‘std’ denotes standard deviation
‘t’ denotes time
‘f’ denotes frequency domain signals
‘BodyAcc’ denotes body linear acceleration
‘GravityAcc’ denotes gravity acceleration
‘BodyGyro’ denotes  angular velocity
